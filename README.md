### About me

Tech Lead at **Yummy (YC S21)**, scaling Open Finance, Core Payments, and AI-driven infrastructure for fintech at startup speed with banking-grade robustness. 10+ years building systems that move real money at scale across LATAM.

**Key highlights:**

- 🏦 Redesigned the core billing system at Yummy — cut +12M invoice processing from **4 days → 15 minutes** (serverless architecture)
- ⚡ Designed the mass banking dispersion engine handling **+36K operations/month**
- 🤖 Pioneered **Generative AI** (Autonomous Agents, RAG, LangChain) for financial operations automation
- 🔐 Led **PCI-DSS compliance** at Yummy: VGS tokenization migration + OWASP audit remediating +100 findings
- 🏗️ Built and mentored engineering cells at Yummy, Wompi, Cencosud, and Acid Labs — including grooming my own successor to lead the payments team independently
- 🌐 Open Banking pioneer at **Wompi (Bancolombia)** — co-built the first payment initiation & account query implementation in Colombia (precursor to Bre-B), supporting **13M+ payers** and **40K+ merchants**

Previously: **Wompi** (Bancolombia) · **Cencosud** ($17.4B) · **SKY Airline** (+13.8M passengers/year) · **Falabella** ($14.7B) · **Acid Labs** (+$32B combined client revenue)

- 📬 Read my engineering & fintech articles → [carrilloapps.substack.com](https://carrilloapps.substack.com)
- 🌍 Portfolio & open-source resources → [carrillo.app/recursos](https://carrillo.app/recursos)
- 📫 **m@carrillo.app**
- ⚡ Hobbies: Ham Radio **HJ4JPC**, Music, Movies & Outdoor walks
